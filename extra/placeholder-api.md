# 🧱 Placeholder API

## General Placeholders

<table data-full-width="true"><thead><tr><th>Placeholder</th><th>Description</th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>%zppro_in_queue%</td><td>Number of players who are currently queueing.</td></tr><tr><td>%zppro_in_queue_&#x3C;ladder>%</td><td>Number of players who are currently queueing for the specified ladder.</td></tr><tr><td>%zppro_in_fight%</td><td>Number of players who are currently fighting.</td></tr><tr><td>%zppro_in_fight_&#x3C;ladder>%</td><td>Number of players who are currently fighting with the specified ladder.</td></tr></tbody></table>

## Player Placeholders

<table data-full-width="true"><thead><tr><th>Rank Placeholder</th><th>Description</th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>%zppro_division_short%</td><td>The short name of the player's divisions.</td></tr><tr><td>%zppro_division_full%</td><td>The full name of the player's division.</td></tr></tbody></table>

<table data-full-width="true"><thead><tr><th>Win Placeholder</th><th>Description</th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>%zppro_wins_global%</td><td>The overall global wins of the player.</td></tr><tr><td>%zppro_wins_global_u%</td><td>The unranked global wins of the players.</td></tr><tr><td>%zppro_wins_global_r%</td><td>The ranked global wins of the players.</td></tr><tr><td>%zppro_wins_ladder_&#x3C;ladder>_u%</td><td>The unranked ladder wins of the player.</td></tr><tr><td>%zppro_wins_ladder_&#x3C;ladder>_r%</td><td>The ranked ladder wins of the player.</td></tr></tbody></table>

<table data-full-width="true"><thead><tr><th>Lose Placeholder</th><th>Description</th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>%zppro_losses_global%</td><td>The overall global losses of the player.</td></tr><tr><td>%zppro_losses_global_u%</td><td>The unranked global losses of the players.</td></tr><tr><td>%zppro_losses_global_r%</td><td>The ranked global losses of the players.</td></tr><tr><td>%zppro_losses_ladder_&#x3C;ladder>_u%</td><td>The unranked ladder losses of the player.</td></tr><tr><td>%zppro_losses_ladder_&#x3C;ladder>_r%</td><td>The ranked ladder losses of the player.</td></tr></tbody></table>

<table data-full-width="true"><thead><tr><th>Elo Placeholder</th><th>Description</th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>%zppro_elo_global%</td><td>The global elo of the player.</td></tr><tr><td>%zppro_elo_ladder_&#x3C;ladder>%</td><td>The ladder elo of the player.</td></tr></tbody></table>

<table data-full-width="true"><thead><tr><th>Leaderboard Placeholder</th><th>Description</th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>%zppro_lb_global_wins_&#x3C;1-10>_k%</td><td>The name of the player who is in the specified position on the global win leaderboard.</td></tr><tr><td>%zppro_lb_global_wins_&#x3C;1-10>_v%</td><td>The number of wins of the player who is in the specified position on the global win leaderboard.</td></tr><tr><td>%zppro_lb_global_elo_&#x3C;1-10>_k%</td><td>The name of the player who is in the specified position on the global elo leaderboard.</td></tr><tr><td>%zppro_lb_global_elo_&#x3C;1-10>_v%</td><td>The elo of the player who is in the specified position on the global win leaderboard.</td></tr><tr><td>%zppro_lb_ladder_&#x3C;ladder>_wins_&#x3C;1-10>_k%</td><td>The name of the player who is in the specified position on the specified ladders win leaderboard.</td></tr><tr><td>%zppro_lb_ladder_&#x3C;ladder>_wins_&#x3C;1-10>_v%</td><td>The number of wins of the player who is in the specified position on the specified ladders win leaderboard.</td></tr><tr><td>%zppro_lb_ladder_&#x3C;ladder>_elo_&#x3C;1-10>_k%</td><td>The name of the player who is in the specified position on the specified ladders elo leaderboard.</td></tr><tr><td>%zppro_lb_ladder_&#x3C;ladder>_elo_&#x3C;1-10>_v%</td><td>The elo of the player who is in the specified position on the specified ladders win leaderboard.</td></tr></tbody></table>

<table data-full-width="true"><thead><tr><th>Group Placeholder</th><th>Description</th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>%zppro_group_name%</td><td>The display name of the players group.</td></tr><tr><td>%zppro_group_prefix%</td><td>The nametag prefix of the players group.</td></tr><tr><td>%zppro_group_suffix%</td><td>The nametag suffix of the players group.</td></tr><tr><td>%zppro_group_limit_u%</td><td>The unranked match limit that the players group is allowed to play.</td></tr><tr><td>%zppro_group_limit_r%</td><td>The ranked match limit that the players group is allowed to play.</td></tr></tbody></table>
